## Tips for Success
1.  **Consistency is Key:** Solve at least 1 problem every day.
2.  **Understand, Don't Memorize:** Focus on the pattern and logic.
3.  **Space Repetition:** Revisit problems after a week or two.
4.  **Mock Interviews:** Practice explaining your thought process out loud.
